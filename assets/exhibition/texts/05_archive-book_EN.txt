Ano Bldg Archive Book / Proposal: Ano Bldg / Opening:


Walking through Nihonbashi Yokoyama-cho, the streetscape reveals dense layers of time.
Small wooden wholesale buildings that continue to operate today. Eight-story buildings with bold ornamentation, built during a period of economic growth. And, more recently, a large-scale redevelopment complex, formed by consolidating these small plots of land.
Fragments of architectural expression from buildings of different historical backgrounds appear sporadically in one’s field of vision, then disappear again.

By containing fragments that recall the surrounding buildings, Ano Bldg enables us to perceive connections again within a streetscape that had seemed fragmented. It gives meaning to the streetscape, while at the same time emerging as architecture through that very streetscape. This is a proposal for a building that grounds its own presence, and that of its surroundings, by intertwining with the relationships already latent in the city.
