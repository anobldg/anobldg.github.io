Season 3: Exhibition as a Medium for Architecture / 
Exhibition Caption:


"Ano BLDG. and Yokoyama-cho"

The third booklet, “Ano bldg and Yokoyama-cho,” describes how Ano bldg was designed with sincerity, without departing from the laws and rules of the city, as well as our own way of engaging with the city. This perspective is also one way of seeing Ano bldg, and remains only a fragment. Observing the elements of the surroundings is close to discovering the rules and order of the city, and incorporating them into the design.
In that sense, the first and third booklets may be deeply connected.
