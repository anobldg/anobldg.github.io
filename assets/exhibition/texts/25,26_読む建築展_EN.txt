Season 3: Exhibition as a Medium for Architecture / 
Exhibition Caption:


“From Fragments to the Whole”

The first booklet, “From Fragments to the Whole,” shows how Ano Bldg, as a single work of architecture, was formed through the reconstruction of its surroundings, history, and many fragments, through both continuity and interruption. Within the booklet, multiple chapters and sections are scattered throughout, and the pages, which change in size from chapter to chapter, gradually shift out of alignment. By deliberately enlarging and emphasizing the misalignment of paper that occurs when the booklet is side-bound, the composition expresses a return to the whole while preserving the ambiguity that exists between fragments.
