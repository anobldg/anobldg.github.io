Season 1: In Search of Abstraction / 
Exhibition Caption:


H. Architecture as Abstraction

Ano Bldg aims to emerge as “architecture as an abstraction of the city.” Behind its form lies an operation of integration carried out through our own way of seeing the world.

Taking the six existing buildings in the surrounding area as our starting point, we observed, photographed, selected, and reconstructed their fragments. The abstraction of these buildings emerges by projecting our point of view through the technology of photography, and by combining and compounding those fragments.

Ano Bldg, formed in this way, has a space that maintains recognizable yet complex relationships without converging into a clear concept. Depending on how it is seen by those who visit or live there, their behavior toward this space may change.

While Ano Bldg has strong formal relationships with the six surrounding buildings, it does not simply imitate them. Instead, it presents its own process of reconstruction to the city.
