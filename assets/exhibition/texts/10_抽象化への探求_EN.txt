D. In Search of Abstraction / From Fragments to a Whole

Video 1: In Search of Abstraction
This video expresses how the six buildings around the site appear with particular richness when seen through the method described in C.
It captures the buildings cinematically, as accumulations of fragments.

Video 2: From Fragments to a Whole
What kinds of lives might emerge from the fragments of Ano Bldg? This is expressed through the layering of two-dimensional still images. The lives of four people intersect within a single building.

Use: Mixed-use building
Character 1: An older man who runs a wholesale business
Character 2: A lively young resident
Character 3: A person who runs a café
Character 4: An office worker
